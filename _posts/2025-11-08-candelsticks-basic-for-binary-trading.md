Each candlestick represents **price movement over a specific time period** (e.g., 1 minute, 5 minutes). It shows:

- **Open**: Price at the start of the candle
- **Close**: Price at the end of the candle
- **High**: Highest price reached
- **Low**: Lowest price reached

### ðŸŸ© Bullish Candle
- Close > Open

### ðŸŸ¥ Bearish Candle
